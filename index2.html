PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Event Ticketing System is a platform that helps users discover, compare, and book events with confidence. Users can browse events, compare prices, read reviews, and purchase tickets in one place. Additionally, business owners can explore venues and send quotations directly without hosting events. The platform bridges the gap between users and venue providers, creating a more efficient and affordable event planning experience.

FRAMING THE EVENT BOOKING EXPERIENCE CHALLENGES

Users often struggle to discover and compare suitable events, leading to a less confident and inefficient booking experience. For business owners, venue discovery is fragmented, making it difficult to find relevant venues and express interest effectively. As a result, communication between users and venue providers can become delayed, affecting the overall efficiency of the booking process.

PROJECT GOALS

  • Design a unified event ticketing platform for event discovery, comparison, and booking
  • Improve user experience by reducing friction in finding and booking events
  • Enable users to confidently compare events using clear and centralized information
  • Simplify venue discovery and quotation requests for business owners and organizers
  • Improve communication between users, businesses, and venue providers
  • Create a connected ecosystem that streamlines the end-to-end event booking process
  • Increase efficiency, transparency, and accessibility across the platform

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

USER RESEARCH

  • Users struggle with scattered event information and poor discoverability
  • Comparing events is difficult, leading to low booking confidence
  • Users prefer a centralized platform with clear filtering and comparison
  • Venue discovery and communication for businesses is slow and fragmented
  • Users expect a simple, fast booking flow with clear pricing and confirmation
  • Lack of transparency reduces trust in existing event platforms

PAIN POINTS
WALKAROUNDS

Time-Consuming Ticket Searches: Manually comparing multiple websites to find available events that fit his busy schedule is exhausting.

He often skips searching and misses out on events or settles for less-than-ideal options to save time.

Complex Checkout Processes: Long, multi-step forms and unstable websites make the purchase process feel tedious and risky.

He abandons his cart immediately if the website is too complicated or experiences technical glitches during purchase.

Hidden Fees: Extra service or convenience fees that appear only at the final checkout stage cause significant frustration and distrust.

He actively avoids platforms that hide costs and prefers services that provide transparent, "all-in" pricing upfront.

DESIGNING INTERACTIVE EXPERIENCE

DISCOVERING EXCITING EVENTS

When he enters the Event Ticket System, he can see featured events, popular venues, and important event information displayed on the Home Page. The system helps him quickly discover upcoming events and navigate to different sections to explore more details and book tickets.

EXPLORING EVENTS EASILY

He can see featured and trending events on the Home Page. By clicking "View All," he goes to the Events Page where he can browse all or trending events. He can then view event details, locations, and venues to choose an event that matches his interests.

EMPOWERING THE USER FROM DISCOVERY TO PURCHASE

The Event Ticket System empowers the user to effortlessly explore event details and secure his tickets directly within the interface, allowing him to make informed decisions and complete his booking in a seamless, stress-free flow without ever needing to leave the page.

QUICK TICKET CHECKOUT

Once he decides to attend, he can enter his details, select a ticket type, and complete the booking in just a few steps. The streamlined checkout process helps him secure his ticket quickly and effortlessly.

SECURE EMAIL VERIFICATION

After submitting the booking details, he verifies his email with a 5-digit OTP. This quick step confirms his identity and allows him to complete the ticket purchase securely.

SECURE TICKET VERIFICATION

After purchasing a ticket, he can upload the QR code to verify its validity. The system instantly displays the ticket and event details, allowing the user to confirm the booking quickly and securely.

ACCESSING VERIFIED TICKET INFORMATION

After uploading the QR code, he can instantly verify the ticket and access all booking details in one place. The dashboard displays event information, schedule, and venue details, allowing the user to confirm registration with confidence. The verified ticket can also be downloaded for easy access on the event day.

EXPLORING EVENT SPACES

As an event organizer, the user can browse available venues and review key details such as capacity, facilities, and location. Access to comprehensive venue information helps in comparing options and selecting the most suitable space for the event.

STREAMLINED VENUE INQUIRY

The event organizer submits a venue inquiry form with event requirements and contact details. This allows the platform to provide tailored information, availability, and pricing for the selected venue.

KEY LEARNING

Through this project, I learned that users value simplicity, transparency, and security when purchasing event tickets. Clear information architecture, streamlined booking flows, and secure verification processes play a crucial role in improving user confidence and overall experience.
